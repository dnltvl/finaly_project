Oriental Poppy (Papaver orientale)

Price $130

These fiery red poppies go by the moniker 'Goliath'. Oriental poppies send long tap roots into the soil, helping them establish a long life in your spring garden. Blooms in excess of 7 inches in diameter.

Hydrangea (Hydrangea spp.)

Price $125

In the world of hydrangea hybridizing, the big blooms just keep getting better. This genus of plants has several species commonly used in landscaping, all of which are famous for having large flowers.

Dinner-plate Dahlia (Dahlia hybrids)

Price $140

The classification term dinner-plate dahlia is used to describe any dahlia variety with large, impressive flowers measuring at least eight inches across. Most commercially available dinner-plate dahlias are hybrid cultivars.

Giant Water Lily (Nymphaeaceae Genera)

Price $130

The plants known as water lilies include about 70 species within at least five different genera. They are found across the world, but those most often sold commercially are tropical species.

Orienpet Lily (Lilium Orania)

Price $125

Hybrids of trumpet and Oriental lilies, orienpet lilies are increasing in popularity as new cultivars continually hit the market. Their large fragrant blooms appear in late summer on sturdy five-foot stems.
